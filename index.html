🔐 Privacy Policy

for Dictionary Bot · Last updated: July 2026

This bot respects your privacy. We do not store any personal data permanently. Below is a clear explanation of what data is collected and why.

📦 What data we collect

  • Chat ID – used to send you messages and store your preferences temporarily.
  • Words you search – stored in memory to show you your history and favorites.
  • Language preference – chosen via the /language command.
  • Favorites list – saved by you with /addfav.

🧠 How we store it

  • All data is kept in memory only (RAM).
  • It is not written to any database or file.
  • Data resets every time the bot restarts or is redeployed.

🔁 How we use it

  • To provide dictionary definitions, synonyms, examples, and voice pronunciation.
  • To save your favorites and search history so you can access them later.
  • To apply your preferred language mode (English→English, English→Persian, or Persian→English).
✅ We never share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.
The only external APIs used are for dictionary lookup, translation, and text‑to‑speech – they receive only the word you query and nothing else.

🗑️ Your control

  • Clear your search history anytime with /clearhistory.
  • Remove individual favorites with /removefav.

📬 Contact

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out via our website.
