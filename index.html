This Privacy Policy explains how Khan Dictionary (the “Bot”) handles your data. We are committed to transparency and protecting your privacy.

Last updated: July 2026

Data We Collect

To provide its core functionality, the Bot temporarily processes the following data:

  • Chat ID – used to route messages and store user preferences temporarily.
  • Queried words – processed to fetch definitions, synonyms, examples, and pronunciation.
  • Favorites & History – saved per user to provide a personalised experience.
  • Language preference – chosen via the /language command.

How We Store Data

  • In‑memory only – all collected data resides exclusively in RAM.
  • No persistent storage – no databases, files, or external logs are used.
  • Auto‑clear – data is automatically discarded upon bot restart or redeployment.

How We Use Data

  • Deliver accurate definitions, examples, and voice pronunciations.
  • Maintain your search history and favorites list for easy access.
  • Apply your preferred language settings (English–English, English–Persian, or Persian–English).

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for marketing purposes. The Bot relies on external APIs to function:

  • Dictionary API – fetches definitions and examples.
  • MyMemory API – provides translations (Persian modes).
  • StreamElements TTS – generates voice pronunciation.
These services receive only the word you query – no personal identifiers are attached.

Your Rights & Control

  • Clear your entire search history with /clearhistory.
  • Remove individual favorites using /removefav.
  • No permanent storage – your data is never retained beyond the bot’s session.
